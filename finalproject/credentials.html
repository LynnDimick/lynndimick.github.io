Credentials of Lynn Dimick - Instructor

Lynn Dimick has been a Instructor teaching Firearms Safety since 2010 and has been a firearms enthusiast for his entire life. Because of his firearms knowledge, mentoring, and compassion his training servives have been requested all over the western United States.

He recertifies every two years with the NRA. He attends multiple training seminars every year to keep his knowledge current and his skills up to date. This training is recognized worldwide as the best possible. He also spends several days each year in the same use of force training that is received and required of law enforcement officers so that he can share with you information that you will not learn otherwise.
